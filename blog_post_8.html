IT TAKES 2

Jakub Mikolajczak has joined Luminous Bits

It’s a firm belief of mine that to build something great, especially in the video game industry, you need to specialize. Game development demands a wide range of skills, and it's rare for one person to do it all with excellence.

That said, a few weeks back I officially teamed up with Jakub (he helped build V1). This is huge for Devoid. Jakub brings technical creativity that will elevate gameplay and accelerate development. Some days, I’d be in the garage all day working on something he could’ve done in 10 lines of code. Those days are over… well, mostly. I still plan to handle some of the less creative programming myself.

So yeah, the future is bright. Or better yet, the future is Luminous.

-Charles
