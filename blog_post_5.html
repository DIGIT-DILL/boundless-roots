MAR 28TH · 2025

V1 IS LIVE!

The first demo version is now available to playtest! Sign up on the homepage to get that bad boy up and running on your machine. It supports Windows and Mac at the moment. You’ll want to play this one with keyboard and mouse for the time being.

There probably should be more satisfaction on my end with this small yet important milestone. But as famous NBA player Kobe Bryant put it after going up 2-0 in the 09’ NBA finals “job’s not finished” and in the case of Devoid we’re just wrapping up the pre-season.

As for my next steps, I'm going to really engage with the demo players and do a deep dive on their feedback. I see this being a crucial moment in this game's journey and I want to give it my full attention.

Also Devoid once again is missing a strong programming presence. So I'll be on the lookout for another collaborator. Ideally someone who is in it for the long haul and wants to set their mark on Devoid.

To cap things off for this blog post I want to give a special shoutout to Jakub for helping me create the demo. Oh, and happy birthday Angie!

Until next time,

- Charles
