MEET YOUR INSTRUCTOR

Jenette Wilkinson, MBA, RYT 200, holds certifications in Pilates and yoga. She is a fully certified Third-Generation Classical Pilates Instructor, having completed the 650-hour comprehensive Pilates certification through The Art of Control under the guidance of Second-Generation Pilates Instructor Simona Cipriani, and Third-Generation Pilates Instructor Nahar Jaleel, P.T.

After years of desk work led to low back and shoulder issues, Jenette rediscovered her passion for yoga and Pilates. Focusing on core strength, shoulder mobility, and mindfulness, these practices became essential to her healing journey. Now dedicated to sharing the transformative benefits of yoga and Pilates, she inspires others to embrace their potential.

In addition to her fitness pursuits, Jenette holds a Bachelor’s degree in Advertising & Graphic Design and a Master’s in Business. Passionate about lifelong learning, she seizes every opportunity to expand her knowledge and continue her education. She resides in the Village of Cambridge, where she teaches movement classes at Hubbard Hall.
